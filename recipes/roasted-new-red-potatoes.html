Roasted New Red Potatoes Recipe

Home

Description

This easy recipe for roasted new red potatoes results in crispy, flavorful bites with tender centers. Tossed in olive oil, garlic, and herbs, these potatoes make the perfect side dish for just about any meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds new red potatoes, quartered
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. In a large bowl, toss the quartered potatoes with olive oil, garlic, salt, basil, parsley, and black pepper until well coated.

  3. Spread the potatoes evenly on a large baking sheet.

  4. Roast in the preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are browned and crisp on the outside and tender inside.

  5. Serve hot as a side dish with your favorite main course.
